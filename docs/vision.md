# Vision & principles

## Mission

Build a political movement in Ghent that restores **liberal economics** and **competent government** as first-class values — and proves they work at city level.

## What "smart" means

We reject both technocratic arrogance and anti-intellectual populism. Smart policy means:

- **Evidence before ideology** — pilot, measure, scale what works
- **Digital-first government** — fewer queues, faster permits, transparent decisions
- **Long-term thinking** — infrastructure, skills, and fiscal buffers over election-cycle gimmicks
- **Honest trade-offs** — we say what things cost and what we will not do

## What "economically liberal" means

In the Belgian/Flemish tradition, this is not American libertarianism. It means:

- **Competition and entrepreneurship** as engines of prosperity and innovation
- **Low, simple, fair taxation** — broad base, minimal exceptions, no punishment of success
- **Labour market flexibility** with a strong safety net ( Danish flexicurity, not race-to-the-bottom )
- **Free trade and open Europe** — Belgium wins by connecting, not protecting
- **Fiscal responsibility** — debt is a tax on the next generation

## What we are not

- Not a culture-war party
- Not anti-environment (markets + pricing solve more than bans)
- Not socialist-lite with liberal branding
- Not a one-issue protest list

## Values

| Value | In practice |
|-------|-------------|
| **Vrijheid** | Individual choice in work, enterprise, and lifestyle — within rule of law |
| **Verantwoordelijkheid** | Rights come with duties; subsidies come with accountability |
| **Competentie** | Hire and promote on merit; reform what fails |
| **Openheid** | Transparent budgets, open data, multilingual accessibility where needed |
| **Eigenaarschap** | Citizens and neighbourhoods co-own solutions, government enables |

## Position relative to existing parties

| Party | What we take | What we reject |
|-------|--------------|----------------|
| **Open VLD** | Liberal heritage, business realism | Vague positioning, fear of reform, federal paralysis |
| **Volt** | European outlook, urban energy, generational appeal | Weak economic programme, sometimes style over substance |
| **N-VA** | Administrative seriousness (sometimes) | Nationalism, economic interventionism |
| **Groen** | Climate urgency | Over-regulation, distrust of markets |
| **Vooruit/sp.a** | Social concern | High-tax default, union capture |

## Target voter

You might be our voter if you:

- Run a business or want to start one in Ghent
- Work in tech, academia, or the creative economy
- Commute between Ghent's neighbourhoods and care about mobility that actually works
- Vote liberal or progressive but feel embarrassed by the options
- Want Ghent to lead Flanders, not follow Brussels or Antwerp
