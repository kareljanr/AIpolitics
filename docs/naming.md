# Naming & brand

Working titles. Criteria: works in **Dutch**, not embarrassing in French/English, domain/handle freeness, not easily cordoned as a joke, signals **truth + freedom + competence**.

## Positioning words (allowed)

truth · vrijheid · welvaart · overvloed · verdienste · eenvoud · DOGE/efficiëntie · rekenen · werken · bouwen

## Positioning words (careful)

AI (use as method, not whole name if it ages badly) · “partij” (boring) · “nieuw” (overused) · pure ethno markers

---

## Shortlist

| Name | Rationale | Risk |
|------|-----------|------|
| **Waarheid** | North star literal | Abstract; ownership hard |
| **RekenPartij / De Rekening** | Arithmetic, bills, accountability | Can sound scold-y |
| **Overvloed** | Abundance framing | Soft without fiscal edge |
| **Vrij & Waar** | Freedom + truth | Two-concept weight |
| **DOGE België** | Instant global meme + cuts brand | Meme decay; US baggage |
| **Efficiënt** | State competence | Cold |
| **Basis** | First principles | Generic |
| **AIpolitics / AI Politiek** | Repo name; novelty | Clown risk; tech-bro |
| **Nultwijn / Nulverlies** | Anti-waste | Obscure |
| **Productief België** | Contribution frame | Exclusion misread |
| **Open Boek** | Transparency | Soft |
| **Libertair Liberals 2.0** names | Clear economics | Crowded with failed splits |

## Tagline candidates

- *Eerst de waarheid. Dan het beleid.*  
- *Overvloed voor wie bouwt. Vangnet voor wie niet kan. Geen circus voor wie niet wil.*  
- *Hoogste lasten, slechtste prikkels — tijd om te rekenen.*  
- *Minder subsidies. Meer welvaart.*  
- *Humans accountable. Machines for checking.*  

## Visual direction

- Clean, high contrast, data-native (charts as brand assets)  
- Avoid copying Open VLD orange, N-VA yellow, VB, Groen  
- Prefer: deep blue/black + single accent (signal, not rainbow soup)  
- Typography > clipart robots  

## Decision process

1. Legal/KBO name search  
2. `.be` + major social handles  
3. Focus group of 20 target voters (net taxpayers)  
4. Stress-test: does the name survive a hostile headline?  
5. Member poll after 50+ committed people  

Until then, public brand can stay **descriptive**: “het waarheids- en efficiëntieproject” / working title **AIpolitics**.
