# Flemish provinces budget 2026 — comparative snapshot

Synthesis only from DOGE ticks 78–82 primary rows. **No invented euros.**

## Caveats

- **Accounting:** Flemish BBC (exploitatie/investering/financiering), not Walloon ordinaire/extraordinaire.
- **West-Vlaanderen:** full exploitatie-uitgaven not text-extracted (M2/T2 image tables); invest period and debt strong; werkingssubsidies ~€55m **medium**.
- **Oost-Vlaanderen:** beleidsverklaring has invest/tax/personnel/debt; **no full exp/cashout** line.
- **Personnel:** OVL and Limburg include large VL-gesubsidieerd onderwijs pass-through; Antwerp/VBR smaller onderwijs share.
- **Cash-out** = exp + invest + financing uitgaven where M2 complete (ANT, LIM, VBR only).
