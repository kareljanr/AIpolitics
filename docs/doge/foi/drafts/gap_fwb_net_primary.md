# FOI draft — gap_fwb_net_primary

**Status:** ready (not sent — human only)  
**Gap:** Official calculation of Fédération Wallonie-Bruxelles (Communauté française) **net primary expenditure** growth 2025–2026 vs HRF path  
**Known:** HRF Apr 2025 sleutel 3 caps Franse Gemeenschap at **+2.75% (2025)** / **+0.74% (2026)** / avg **1.52% (2025–31)**. Cour des comptes (budget ajusté 2025 FWB commission): exposé général **does not report** dépenses primaires nettes evolution vs CSF 2.75%. DGBF éléments-clés 2026 give totals (dep €15.407 bn / SEC −€1.608 bn) but **not** net-primary growth. Government acknowledged gap and postponed detailed comparison (joint WAL–FWB path narrative). Gross dépenses growth is **not** a substitute metric.
