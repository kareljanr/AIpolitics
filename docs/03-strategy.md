# 03 — Strategy: from zero to power

**North star:** truth + abundance.  
**Constraint:** Belgian institutions (PR, coalitions, language split, party finance).  
**Tone:** ambitious, not delusional. Crazy *methods*; sober *math*.

## Victory definition

Not “become prime minister in 24 months.” That is fanfic.

**Realistic ladder:**

| Level | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| L1 Agenda setter | Media + opposition parties forced to answer your leaderboard |
| L2 Ballot real | Lists filed; >1% in target areas |
| L3 Kingmaker seed | 1–5% Flanders; seats in local councils |
| L4 Parliamentary actor | Seats in Flemish and/or federal parliament; public funding |
| L5 Coalition blackmail | Cannot form majority without your fiscal package |
| L6 Executive power | Ministers implement DOGE + tax reform for real |

Each level has different tactics. Do not run L6 messaging at L1 capacity.

## The crazy strategy (coherent)

### Core idea: **Truth Stack**

Stack three things that alone are weak and together are dangerous:

1. **DOGE Belgium** — permanent public leaderboard of waste and perverse incentives  
2. **AI-augmented policy factory** — every proposal scored, simulated, falsifiable  
3. **Net-taxpayer coalition** — organise the people who fund the system and currently feel unrepresented by both soft liberals and pure identity parties  

Legacy parties sell identity or clienteles. You sell **arithmetic that cannot be unseen**.

### Why this can work in Belgium specifically

| Feature | Exploit |
|---------|---------|
| Highest labour tax wedge in OECD class | Unavoidable pocketbook issue |
| Deficit + debt trajectory | Elite anxiety + EU pressure = reform windows |
| Fragmented party system | Small vote shares can still yield seats / leverage |
| Signature ballot access | No US-style party machine monopoly on existence |
| Media hunger for novelty | “AI party” earns coverage money cannot buy |
| Distrust of classic parties | Permission structure for a new brand |

### Why this can fail

| Failure | Mitigation |
|---------|------------|
| Clown framing (“chatbot party”) | Humans own liability; AI is method; publish substance weekly |
| Cordons / exclusion | Stay legal, non-violent, evidence-first; make exclusion look anti-truth |
| Zero ground game | Signature army + local beachheads first |
| Capture by single issue | Keep tax/work/housing/safety package integrated |
| Founder burnout | Institutionalise: board, rotating spokespeople, open repo |

## Phase plan

### Phase 0 — Doctrine & assets (now → 3 months)

**Goals**

- Freeze north star + red lines  
- Public DOGE leaderboard v0 (top 25 items, even if partial €)  
- Manifesto NL v0.9  
- Core team of 8+  
- Legal memo on VZW + first target election  

**KPIs**

- 10 high-quality docs (this repo)  
- 1,000 newsletter / Telegram / X followers who are *real* (not bots)  
- 3 journalists who know your name as “the waste leaderboard people”

### Phase 1 — Attention engine (3–12 months)

See [08-media-attention.md](08-media-attention.md).

**Weekly ritual (non-negotiable)**

1. Publish **one** leaderboard update or “subsidy autopsy”  
2. Publish **one** falsified assumption (yours or theirs)  
3. Answer critics with data, not purity spirals  

**Products**

- “Belgium Waste Index”  
- Tax wedge calculator for average worker  
- “If parties were companies” budget dashboard  

**Tone control**

- Sharp > crude  
- Mechanisms > scapegoats  
- Numbers > adjectives  

### Phase 2 — Organisation (parallel, months 3–18)

- VZW live  
- Provincial captains (Flanders)  
- Signature CRM (legal, GDPR)  
- Candidate school: media, law, fiscal literacy  
- Local listening tours in entrepreneur-dense communes  

### Phase 3 — First electoral test

Pick **one** primary arena:

**Option A — Local 2030 (depth)**  
Build mayors, dossiers, competence story. Slow, strong.

**Option B — Regional/federal next cycle (speed)**  
Higher risk, higher media, needs money discipline and star candidates.

**Option C — Hybrid**  
Run limited federal constituencies where signature capacity is real; go wide only where organisers exist.

**Decision rule:** only contest constituencies where you can (1) fill a gender-compliant list and (2) collect signatures with margin.

### Phase 4 — Seats → funding → machine

Once seats exist:

- Public funding professionalises staff  
- Study centre VZW produces bills  
- Parliamentary questions feed the leaderboard  
- Never let funding turn you into a jobs programme for activists

### Phase 5 — Power

See [07-coalitions-negotiation.md](07-coalitions-negotiation.md).

You enter government only with a **written fiscal compact**:

- multi-year primary balance path  
- labour tax wedge reduction schedule  
- DOGE cut list with euro targets  
- migration/contribution rules  
- kill switches if partners violate compact  

No compact → opposition with a megaphone.

## Voter strategy

### Primary targets (rank order)

1. **Net taxpayers under 50** — employees and freelancers crushed by wedge  
2. **SME owners / scale-ups** — regulation + tax horror  
3. **High-skill immigrants and expats** who see Belgium’s inefficiency clearly  
4. **Students in STEM / economics** — long-term cadre  
5. **Security-anxious urban voters** who want liberalism *with* order  

### Secondary

- Soft Open VLD / MR refugees  
- Fiscal N-VA voters disappointed by coalition mush  
- Non-voters who cite “all the same”  

### Not primary (do not build identity around)

- Culture-war maximalists who reject evidence when inconvenient  
- Transfer-max coalitions that require denying C1–C5 in the diagnosis  

### Message hierarchy

1. **You are being looted by complexity** (taxes + subsidies + deficit)  
2. **Incentives beat sermons**  
3. **Safety net yes, free-rider machine no**  
4. **We publish the books; they publish posters**  
5. AI as proof of method, not Messiah
